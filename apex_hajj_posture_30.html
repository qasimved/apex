Your 3 Dysfunctions — Fix Order
⚠️ Fix order is non-negotiable. APT is the root. Rounded shoulders cannot fully resolve while the pelvis is anteriorly tilted. Forward head cannot fully resolve while the thoracic spine is kyphotic. Bottom up.
01
Anterior Pelvic Tilt (APT)
Weeks 1–2 primary focus · maintained all 4 weeks
ROOT CAUSE
Driver: Hip flexors chronically shortened by desk sitting. Glutes neurologically inhibited.
Visible effect: Lower back arches, belly pushes forward, pelvic floor strain.
Your fix: Hip flexor stretch (2× daily) · Glute activation circuit · Posterior pelvic tilt drills
02
Rounded Shoulders + Thoracic Kyphosis
Weeks 2–3 primary focus · maintained all 4 weeks
MID CHAIN
Driver: Pec minor + anterior delt overactive. Rear delts/mid-traps/rhomboids weak and lengthened.
Visible effect: Shoulders rolled forward, chest caves in, mid-back rounded.
Your fix: Face pulls + pull-aparts daily · Thoracic extension · Scapular retraction drills
03
Forward Head Posture (FHP)
Weeks 3–4 primary focus · builds on fixes 1 & 2
TOP CHAIN
Driver: Upper traps + SCM overactive. Deep neck flexors (longus colli) atrophied.
Visible effect: Head sits forward of shoulders. Neck tension. Headaches.
Your fix: Chin tucks 3× daily · Thoracic extension chair · No upper trap overloading

Phase Detail
WEEK 1 · DAYS 1–7
Foundation — APT Root Fix
Primary: Anterior Pelvic Tilt · Secondary: Begin shoulder awareness
01
🎯 Week 1 goal: Wake the glutes up and start decompressing the hip flexors. These muscles have been in a shortened, inhibited state for years. Expect mild DOMS in glutes by day 2–3. That is the correction starting. Do not skip rest.
🌅
Morning Correctives — Week 1
5–6 min · after Fajr
1
Wall Pelvic Tilt mandatory
5 × 10-sec hold
Back flat against wall. Brace abs + squeeze glutes simultaneously. Feel lower back press into wall — that gap closing is APT correcting. Do this every single morning of all 4 weeks.
2
Glute Bridge mandatory
3 × 15, 2-sec hold at top · BW or Gritin Medium loop
Lie on back, knees bent. Drive through heels — if lower back fires instead of glutes, tuck pelvis harder before lifting. Hold at top, squeeze hard. Progress to Gritin Medium loop band across hips by day 3.
Gritin Light → Gritin Medium by day 3
3
Clamshell
2 × 15 each side · Gritin Light
Lie on side, knees at 45°. Rotate top knee up without rolling the hips back. Hip abductors and external rotators — key APT muscles. Gritin Light for Week 1, no heavier.
Gritin Light loop
4
Chin Tuck (start Week 1)
3 × 10, 3-sec hold
Standing or sitting. Pull head straight back — "make a double chin." Ears move over shoulders. Do not tilt chin down. Feel deep front neck muscles engage. Build the habit now even though FHP is not primary focus yet.
🌙
Evening Correctives — Week 1
7–8 min · after Isha or before sleep
1
Kneeling Hip Flexor Stretch mandatory · daily all 4 weeks
60 sec each side — do NOT rush
One knee on ground, other foot forward. Push hips forward, tuck tailbone to deepen. You should feel a deep pull at the front of the rear hip/thigh. The heat of Makkah makes this 30% more effective — use it. This single stretch done daily will do more for your back pain and belly appearance than anything else.
2
Couch Stretch
30 sec each side
Rear shin against wall, front foot on floor. Hits rectus femoris — the hip flexor most shortened by chairs. Progress to 45 sec by end of Week 1.
3
90/90 Hip Stretch
60 sec each side
Sit with both knees at 90°, front shin across, rear shin behind. Upright torso. Opens hip external rotators and capsule. New addition for Hajj — the walking volume creates hip tightness this directly addresses.
4
Band Pull-Apart mandatory
2 × 20 · Yellow tube band
Arms straight at shoulder height. Pull band to T-shape, squeeze scapulae at end. Yellow only — near-zero fatigue cost. Primes rear delts and mid-traps. Start this habit Week 1, even though shoulder focus comes Week 2.
Yellow tube band (4.6kg)
🚶 Hajj walking: Every tawaf, every sa'i, every walk between hotels is active rehab. Focus cues during walking: chest lifted · shoulders back · chin tucked · glutes squeezing slightly on each push-off. This turns 20,000 steps into 20,000 reps of postural reinforcement.

Add face pulls and band rows to begin building the posterior shoulder strength needed to hold the corrected position.

8–10 min · after Fajr

5 × 10-sec hold
Same as Week 1. Non-negotiable daily for all 30 days. By now this should feel natural rather than effortful — that's the neural pattern solidifying.

Glute Bridge mandatory
3 × 15, 2-sec hold · Gritin Medium loop
Progress from Week 1. Gritin Medium loop band placed across hip creases. Drive through heels. Band adds hip abduction resistance — activates glute med simultaneously. If lower back still compensating → single leg BW instead.

2 × 20 each side · Gritin Medium
Progress from Gritin Light to Gritin Medium. If 20 reps feel easy, pause 2 seconds at top of each rep before progressing band level.
Gritin Medium loop
4
Scapular Wall Slides NEW Week 2
2 × 10 slow reps
Back flat on wall, arms at 90° ("goalpost"). Slide arms up wall keeping entire arm in contact. If arms peel off, reduce range. Targets lower traps and serratus — the muscles that hold shoulder blades in correct position. Do slowly — 3 sec up, 2 sec down.
5
Thoracic Extension — Chair/Wall NEW Week 2
2 × 60 sec
Lean back over the top of a chair at mid-thoracic level (NOT lower back). Arms crossed on chest or behind head. Let gravity extend the thoracic curve. You should feel a gentle stretch across the upper back. This is thoracic kyphosis reversing. Can also do with rolled towel on floor.
6
Chin Tuck
3 × 10, 3-sec hold — add one more set
Now do 3 sets every morning. By end of Week 2 this should feel like a natural resting position for 1–2 seconds.

Band Session — Week 2 · Every Other Day
15–20 min · cooler time of day preferred
Do on alternate days — not after heavy tawaf/walking days. If energy ≤ 4 → skip and just do morning + evening correctives.
1
Band Face Pull mandatory every session
3 × 15 · Yellow band
Door anchor at eye height. Face door. Pull to forehead, elbows flared wide and high. Add chin tuck at full contraction — this trains the FHP fix simultaneously. Yellow band keeps fatigue cost near zero. Do EVERY band session all 4 weeks.
Yellow tube band (4.6kg)
2
Band Pull-Apart mandatory every session
3 × 20 · Yellow band
Standing, arms straight at shoulder height. Pull to T-shape, hold 1 sec. Rear delts + mid-traps + rhomboids. Volume here is the posture correction stimulus — 3×20 every session adds up to hundreds of reps per week.
Yellow tube band (4.6kg)
3
Band Single-Arm Row
3 × 12 each side · Red band
Foot on band, hinge 45° at hip, row to hip. Elbow drives back, not up. Feel mid-back contract. Posterior pelvic tilt maintained — do not let lower back arch as you pull. Row = back posture fix. Red band keeps load manageable in heat.
Red tube band (13.6kg)
4
Band Lateral Walk
3 × 15 steps each direction · Gritin Medium
Gritin loop around ankles. Slight squat position. Step side to side — keep pelvis level, do NOT hike hip. Glute med activation is critical for APT and pelvic stability during walking.
Gritin Medium loop
5
Band Bicep Curl (retention)
3 × 12 · Green band
Stand on band, standard curl. Supinate (rotate palm up) at top. This is muscle retention, not primary focus. Green band is correct level here — not fatiguing.
Green tube band (9.0kg)

Return to Munich — First Week Back
✅ You will return to the Munich gym with: stronger glutes · longer hip flexors · improved thoracic mobility · stronger rear delts and mid-traps · better deep neck flexor tone. The desk job will immediately begin working against this — but now you have the corrective protocol fully habituated.
  • Keep the morning + evening correctives permanently — 7–10 min daily. These are now part of your programme, not a temporary fix.
  • Face pulls + pull-aparts mandatory every upper body gym session — written into your APEX system prompt (Part 13).
  • First gym session back: reduce all weights ~20%. Tendons adapt slower than muscles — 4 weeks of deload means a deload-style return. Build back over 2 weeks.
  • Hip flexor stretch mandatory after every gym session — the desk job reloads hip flexors daily. Non-negotiable.
  • Log Day 30 posture photo into APEX notes — use it as your baseline for the 4-Week Review.
  • Continue chin tucks 3× daily at desk — set a phone alarm for Dhuhr time as a midday reminder habit carry-over.
